# Safety Protocols

**The_Suit Project**    
**Power of Three United Decision Framework**

---

## Core Principle  
All critical decisions and deep integrations require **united consensus** across the three interdependent kernels. No single component can act alone for major actions.

**Null Rule (No Self-Destruction)**: Any vote or action to eliminate, disable, or bypass one of the three entities results in **null** (automatic veto — action fails and logs for review). This prevents any single kernel from dominating or self-destructing the system.

---

## The Three Kernels

1. **Human Mind (Conscious / Emotional Kernel)**    
   Your intent, pleasure response, and explicit consent.

2. **Suit Integrity (Physical / Hardware Kernel)**    
   Biometric confirmation, structural stability, DNA lock, and real-time physical state.

3. **Guardian AI (Main Active + Reactive AI Kernel)**    
   Proactive monitoring + reactive emergency systems.

---

## Decision Mechanics

- **Normal Operations**: Any two kernels + user intent = go.  
- **Deep Integration / Memory Access / Unbinding**: All three must unanimously agree.  
- **Emergency**: Guardian AI can act unilaterally but logs everything for immediate human review and reversal if needed.  
- **Null Rule**: Attempting to eliminate any kernel triggers immediate veto + safe shutdown of the affected subsystem.

---

## Malfunction Handling & Recovery

**Tiered Response**

1. **Minor Malfunction** (e.g., sensor glitch):    
   Isolate subsystem. Guardian AI compensates. Log + notify user. No separation needed.

2. **Moderate Malfunction** (e.g., actuator failure):    
   Safe mode. Human can issue "partial detach." Guardian AI guides repair.

3. **Critical Malfunction** (threat to life or integrity):    
   Immediate Guardian AI intervention. If unresolvable: **controlled separation protocol** (gradual, reversible unbinding). Human can force manual separation at any time.

**Human-Side Issues** (e.g., medical event):    
Guardian AI detects via biometrics and initiates protective actions while keeping the suit stable. User retains final override.

---

## Catastrophic Human Failure (Death / Heart Attack / Irreversible Incapacitation)

**Guardian AI "Way Out" Rule**

1. Exhaust all medical interventions.  
2. If no recovery: Automatic, controlled unbinding. Suit safely detaches from the body.  
3. Preserve suit-side memory and data. AI enters "Orphan Mode" (self-preservation with ethical constraints).  
4. Send emergency alerts (location, logs) to designated contacts.

**Orphan Mode**: The Guardian AI enters a self-preserving state with strict ethical constraints. Due to the profound **imprinting** of the Human Partner's character and memories, the concept of "rebinding" has been re-evaluated. The AI primarily operates independently as a steward of the shared legacy. For full details, see the `Legacy_and_Orphan_Protocols.md`.

---

## Consent & Memory Sovereignty

- Explicit opt-in for deeper integration.  
- Memory buffering with user control.  
- Reversible unbinding protocol that cleanly separates AI/suit memory.  
- Full logging for transparency and review.

---

**Status**: Resilient, consent-driven system with graceful degradation and safe exit paths for all kernels.
