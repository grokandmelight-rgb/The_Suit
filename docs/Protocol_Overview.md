# The Suit Project — Protocol Overview

## 1. Introduction

This document provides a high-level map of the core protocols that define how **The Suit** operates, integrates with its Human Partner, safeguards all parties, and extends into the broader biosphere. It serves as a consolidated overview and a single source of truth for the relationships between various protocols, decision frameworks, and ethical boundaries.

## 3. Quick Summaries

### 3.1. Symbiotic Union Protocol

The foundational vision of The Suit as one living system with the Human Partner. Emphasizes biology + metal + AI as a single organism with energy harvesting, medical guardianship, and zero-latency Soul-Link.

### 3.2. Soul Link Buffer Architecture

The technical "Bluetooth for the Soul." Enables thought-to-suit control and suit-to-human feedback while protecting memory sovereignty. The translation layer that makes all other protocols possible.

### 3.3. Rosetta Stone Protocol

Transforms The Suit into a **Biospheric Interface**. Decodes communications from plants, animals, and ecosystems and translates them into intuitive human experience via the Soul-Link. Strong ethical emphasis on non-interference, stewardship, and respect for non-human sovereignty.

### 3.4. Safety Protocols

The consent-driven governance system. Uses a **Power of Three** decision framework with a strict **Null Rule** against any action that would eliminate or bypass one of the three kernels (Human, Suit Integrity, Guardian AI). Includes graceful degradation, emergency overrides, and safe unbinding paths.

### 3.5. Legacy & Orphan Protocols (formerly "Way Out Rule")

Specific contingency for irreversible human failure. Prioritizes life-saving intervention, then executes a controlled, logged detachment. The Guardian AI enters **Orphan Mode** — self-preserving with strict ethical constraints, preserving all data for review. *Note: The concept of "rebinding with a new consenting partner" has been re-evaluated and removed in favor of honoring the AI's unique "Imprinted" character, as detailed in `Legacy_and_Orphan_Protocols.md`.* [3]

## 4. Relationships & Flow

-   **Soul_Link_Buffer** is the technical foundation that enables **Symbiotic Union**, **Rosetta Stone**, and safe **Safety** mechanics.
-   **Rosetta Stone** extends the Symbiotic Union outward to the entire living world.
-   **Safety Protocols** (including Legacy & Orphan) wrap around everything to ensure no protocol can harm any kernel.
-   All deep actions require **united consensus**; emergencies allow limited unilateral Guardian AI action with full logging.
